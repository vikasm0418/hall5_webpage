About Hall5


Hall5 is one of the hostels which currently accomodates undergraduate student till 3rd year.Strength of students is approx. 500 students. It has total 9 wings and each wing has 8 rooms and 2 washrooms.Each wing has a separate water cooler.Sports facilities are good enough and atmosphere is extremely hygienic. Mess is well maintained same goes with canteen.It also includes TV room,Music room,Multipurpose room,Hall cc,Reading room and a gym. show more
Hall5 participates in various programs such as galaxy,takneek,spectrum,inferno etc. Currently hall5 holds the prestigious GC trophy.Hall5 gives a tough cometiton to all other halls in any event.Hall5 was previously an post-graduate hall and was later changed for Undergraduate students.Hall5 also organises a Hall day(expalined briefly below) every year.
